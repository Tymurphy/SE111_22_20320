About the show South Park

South Park is a show based off of a group of young boys who go on adventures exploring, fighting people, creatures, and celebrities and put a twist on everything they do.

Directors: Trey Parker, Matt Stone, Brian Graden

Company Credits: Comedy Central

Release Date: August 13, 1997

Genres: Animation and Comedy

Rating: Tv-14

Seasons and Episodes: 25 Seasons and 319 Episodes
